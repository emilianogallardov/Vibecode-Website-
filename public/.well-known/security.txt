# Reporting Security Issues

We take security seriously. If you discover a security vulnerability, please:

1. Email us at security@example.com with details
2. Allow us a reasonable time to respond before public disclosure
3. Do not exploit the vulnerability beyond verification
4. Do not access or modify user data

# Scope

In scope:
- All subdomains of example.com
- Mobile applications
- API endpoints

Out of scope:
- Denial of Service (DoS/DDoS)
- Social engineering attacks
- Physical attacks
- Reports from automated tools without validation

# Safe Harbor

We will not pursue legal action against security researchers who:
- Make good faith efforts to comply with this policy
- Only access their own accounts for testing
- Do not intentionally harm users or our systems
- Do not violate privacy or destroy data

Thank you for helping keep our users safe!
